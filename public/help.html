NFL Schedule — Help
Spoiler-free by default. You control what you see.
Spoilers

Scores are hidden by default. Turn on Show scores in Filters whenever you're ready to see them. Completed games show "Final" until you toggle scores on.

Mark a game as watched with the ○ / ✓ button on any Final card. Use Hide watched to clean up your list.

Schedule view

Games are grouped by NFL week — Preseason, Week 1–18, Wild Card, Divisional, Conference Championship, Super Bowl. The date range for each week is shown next to the heading.

During the off-season (April–August), a countdown card shows when the next season kicks off.

Primetime badges

TNF Thursday Night Football (Amazon Prime) · SNF Sunday Night Football (NBC) · MNF Monday Night Football (ESPN/ABC)

Conference & Division filters

Filter by AFC or NFC, then narrow further by Division (East, West, North, South). Combine them — e.g. AFC + North shows only AFC North division games.

My Team

Open Filters → Favorite teams, star your team, then tap ☆ My Team to set it. Your team's colors take over the app, your games are highlighted, and the team crest appears in the header.

Teams view & rosters

Switch to Teams to browse all 32 clubs organized by conference and division. Tap any team to open its roster. Tap any player for their profile and recent game stats (QB, RB, WR, TE position-specific).

You can also open rosters from the Schedule view by tapping the Roster pill on any game card.

Saving players

Tap ★ on any player in a roster to save them. Open Filters → Players to see your saved players list and jump straight to their profiles.

Saving games

Tap ★ on any game card to bookmark it. Use Filters → Saved to see only your bookmarked games.

Archive

Games older than 21 days roll into the archive. Enable Show archive in Filters to see the full history.

Time zones

All times default to your device's local time. Change the time zone in Filters → the dropdown at the bottom of the filter panel.

Data & updates

Schedule data comes from the ESPN public API and refreshes automatically every 10 minutes. Tap Refresh in the header to force an immediate update. During game days the scraper keeps live scores current.
